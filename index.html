Practicing Areas

Since 1993, Bill has helped hundreds of individuals from all walks of life. Whatever legal issue you might be going through, Bill can help.

Workers' Compensation

If you have been injured on the job, it does not mean you automatically qualify for workers' compensation. Numerous factors are used to determine whether or not you qualify for workers' compensation, including, but not limited to where, when and in what capacity you were working when injured.

Bill is experienced in workers' compensation claims. He understands the qualification requirements and how to work with the insurance companies to get you the benefits that you deserve.

Bill has the necessary experience and knowledge to help you with this complicated process. He understands the frustations that come with needing to take care of yourself and family while trying to heal from an injury. Help is only a phone call away.

Personal Injury

If you have been hurt due to the negligence of someone else, let Bill lighten your burden. An accident can be debilitating and no amount of money could possibly reverse the pain and suffering you or a loved one have encountered. However, financial compensation can lessen the weight of healthcare costs and lost wages.

Bill can assure you that your attention and your thoughts are fully with you or your loved one and not with banks, insurance companies, and debt collectors. The laws are very specific regarding each type of personal injury case. Bill has the experience and legal understanding to negotiate claims for property damage, lost wages, medical costs and psychological pain to the full extent of the law.
